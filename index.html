Against Monitoring and Regulation

  • Free Speech Concerns: Risks of infringing on individuals' rights to express themselves.
  • Privacy Issues: Potential invasions of personal privacy. The goal is to monitor the bad and the ugly. It's a gross abuse of power to monitor random people and invade. It would be like if the third amendment was revoked.
  • Innovation Stifling: Over-regulation can hinder new ideas. People will be afarid to make anything when everything is put under scrutiny. A lot of people make content because its their hobby. If people were forced to follow strict regulations, it could deter very creative people from making any content at all.
